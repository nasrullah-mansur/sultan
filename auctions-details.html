Since 1994, the International Studio & Curatorial Program (ISCP) has welcomed over 1,500 residents from 85 countries to its residency program, offering a platform for the creative development and exchange between artists and curators. In a time when creative practice and critical dialogue are more important than ever, help further ISCP’s mission to build and sustain a vibrant community of contemporary art practitioners and diverse audiences.

Support ISCP by bidding on this year’s auction featuring over 50 works by ISCP’s international alumni and supporters, including Josef Albers, Arianna Carossa, Amanda Church, Conrad Egyir, Pablo Helguera, Nadja Verena Marcin, James Rosenquist, Richard Tsao, Martha Tuttle, Carrie Mae Weems, Terry Winters, and many more.

Bidding is open here, exclusively on Artsy, until Friday, September 11, 2020 at 1:00pm EDT. Artworks will ship directly from the artists’ studio or donors’ location.

On Thursday, September 10, 2020, from 6:30-7:15 PM EST, joins ISCP on Zoom for Celebrate the Studio, a virtual event for ISCP's 2020 Benefit Auction, with guest of honor Martine Gutierrez. RSVP here.
